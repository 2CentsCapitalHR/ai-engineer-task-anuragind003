ADGM Companies Regulations 2020, Article 6: The jurisdiction for companies incorporated in ADGM shall be the ADGM Courts, and disputes shall be governed under ADGM regulations unless otherwise specified in accordance with ADGM law.

ADGM guidance on corporate documentation: Company incorporation documents should include Articles of Association, Memorandum of Association, Board or Shareholder resolutions as applicable, UBO declaration forms, and a Register of Members and Directors.

Signing and execution: Corporate documents should include clear signatory blocks including authorized signatory name, title, date, and signature.
